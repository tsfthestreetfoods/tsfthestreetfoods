How it started

The Street Foods™ started with one simple idea: make local favorites unforgettable with quality, hygiene, and consistency. From one counter to multiple outlets, we have grown because of your trust and love.

Today we proudly serve delicious varieties—from chai-time comfort to quick Chinese cravings—while keeping the same soul in every dish.
